Zichuan Liu | 刘子川

I am a master student at Nanjing University, supervised by Prof. Chunlin Chen. I am currently working as a research intern at DAMO Academy of Alibaba Group, where I am advised by Dr. Qingsong Wen and I am privileged to work closely with Dr. Tianchun Wang, A/Prof. Dongsheng Luo, and A/Prof. Mengnan Du. Previously, I completed my B.S. in Computer Science at Wuhan University of Technology, where I worked with A/Prof. Rui Zhang.

My research interests focus on Time Series, Reinforcement Learning, and eXplainable Artificial Intelligence, especially with applications in multiple sequences, offline models, and dynamic environments, allowing them to explicitly adapt to the non-stationary task distributions in real-world.

I am always open to collaborate with others! Please feel free to email me if you have any questions, or if you are interested in a research collaboration.
